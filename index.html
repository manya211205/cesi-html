About CESI

CESI at IIM Kozhikode serves as a hub for interdisciplinary research and innovation aimed at tackling complex societal challenges. We bring together scholars, practitioners, and policymakers to co-create actionable knowledge and drive inclusive development.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to drive transformative social impact through rigorous research, cross-sectoral collaboration, and inclusive innovation. CESI envisions a society where sustainable and equitable development is catalyzed by academic leadership.
